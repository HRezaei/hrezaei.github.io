About

I'm a PhD student at AI group of the CS department, University of York. My research area is Natural Language Processing (NLP) in general and dialogue systems in particular. I'm interested in improving language understanding of large language model chatbots. I have been also working as a web developer since 2013. But start of my journey with computer and programming dates back to 2004 or so.

I love computer in general, but among this vast realm of amazing concepts, Natural Language Processing has engaged me the most. However, the majority of my proffessional experience has been spent in the Web Development. Indeed, my work has been in the intersection of these two.

You most probably have heard of ChatGPT and other AI chatbots. My passion is understanding them better, improving their language understanding, and hopefully, making their internals more understandable! These are, from my point of view, the inevitable steps towards Robust AI.
